Welcome to Tasting Treats

It is easy to pre-order your cupcakes! For any of our cupcakes "Hot out of the Oven" we need a 24 hour notice. For any of our Special Order Cupcakes we request a 48 hour notice. Simply fill out the form below, click the "Send to our Bakery" It will be faxed directly to our store! You will be contacted shortly to confirm your cupcake order request and payment information.
