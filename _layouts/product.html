Product Care

All cupcakes are shipped frozen to guarantee freshness. Before serving, please let cupcakes stand at room temperature for 3-4 hours. Tasty Treats baked goods are meant to be enjoyed as soon as possible. If you plan to store them we recommend placing them in an airtight container at room temperature. Do not refrigerate or refreeze Tasty Treats cupcakes. Tasty Treats makes everything fresh from scratch in small batches throughout the day, using only the finest ingredients. We bake the old-fashioned way, without the use of preservatives or stabilizers.
